About Global Bridge Export

Founded in 2012, Global Bridge Export International Trading Co., Ltd. is a professional international trading company dedicated to connecting buyers with trusted manufacturers and suppliers across China and international markets.

Our company specializes in product sourcing, procurement, OEM & ODM manufacturing, import & export, quality inspection, logistics coordination and complete supply chain management. We help businesses reduce procurement costs while maintaining high quality standards and reliable delivery.

🎯 Our Mission

To connect businesses worldwide with trusted manufacturers through reliable sourcing, procurement and international trade solutions.

👁 Our Vision

To become one of the world's most trusted international sourcing and export companies by delivering value, quality and long-term partnerships.

💎 Our Core Values

Integrity, Quality, Reliability, Professionalism, Transparency and Customer Satisfaction.
