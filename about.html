About Global Bridge Export

Global Bridge Export International Trading Co., Ltd was established in 2012 as a professional international trading company dedicated to connecting businesses with trusted manufacturers, suppliers and global markets. We serve clients across multiple industries by providing reliable sourcing and trade solutions.



Our core services include global product sourcing, procurement support, import and export operations, logistics coordination, supplier management and supply chain solutions. We work closely with trusted partners to ensure quality, efficiency and competitive pricing.



With a strong supplier network and years of international trade experience, Global Bridge Export helps businesses reduce sourcing risks, improve operational efficiency and expand into international markets with confidence. Our commitment is to build long-term business relationships based on trust, professionalism and reliability.



Our Mission

To create reliable connections between global buyers and trusted suppliers while delivering efficient sourcing, procurement and international trade solutions that support long-term business growth.
