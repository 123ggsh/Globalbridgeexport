About Global Bridge Export

Global Bridge Export International Trading Co., Ltd was founded in 2012 to connect businesses with trusted suppliers, manufacturers and international markets.



We specialize in global sourcing, procurement support, logistics coordination, supply chain management and international trade services. Our mission is to build reliable bridges between buyers and suppliers worldwide.



With years of experience and a strong supplier network, we help businesses reduce sourcing risks, improve efficiency and expand into global markets.
