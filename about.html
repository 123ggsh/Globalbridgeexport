About Global Bridge Export

Building trusted partnerships through professional international sourcing, procurement and global trade solutions since 2012.

Company Overview

Founded in 2012, Global Bridge Export International Trading Co., Ltd is a professional international trading and sourcing company dedicated to helping businesses connect with trusted manufacturers and suppliers across China and Asia.

We specialize in product sourcing, procurement, OEM & ODM manufacturing, export coordination, logistics management and supply chain solutions. Our goal is to provide reliable, cost-effective and efficient trade services that help our clients succeed in the global marketplace.

Our Mission

To build long-term business partnerships by providing dependable sourcing, procurement and international trade services while ensuring quality, transparency and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become one of the world's most trusted international sourcing and trading partners, connecting businesses with reliable manufacturers and creating lasting value through global commerce.
